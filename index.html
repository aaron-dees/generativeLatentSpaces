Generative Latent Spaces for Neural Snythesis of Audio Textures - Audio Samples

Aaron Dees, Sean O'Leary
Technological University of Dublin

In this paper we evaluate and investigate the audio texture synthesis capabilities and generative latent spaces of two paradigms in neural audio synthesis, using Variational Autoencoders (VAEs). We introduce datasets for three categories of environmental sounds for use in machine learning. We propose VAE frameworks with fine grained temporal control, for both DSP-inspired and data driven approaches to neural audio synthesis, of audio textures. We evaluate and compare the the models synthesis capabilities via objective metrics. We investigate the expressive and generative capabilities of each of the models through latent space navigation and interpolations.
